Pricing Decision Simulator

An interactive market experiment

In this simulation, you are a seller trying to close a deal with a buyer. The buyer has a private valuation for the product — a maximum price they are willing to pay.

You will make three pricing attempts. For each attempt, enter the price you would quote to the buyer. After submitting, you will see whether each quote resulted in a sale.

A sale occurs only when the price you quote does not exceed what the buyer is willing to pay.
